Nitrous Oxide For Nervous Patients

Nitrous Oxide Sedation, often known as “laughing gas,” is a safe, mild sedative that helps reduce anxiety during dental procedures. It's ideal for children, teens, and adults who experience dental fear or nervousness.

The gas is inhaled through a small nose mask and takes effect within minutes, helping you feel calm and comfortable. The best part? The effects wear off quickly after your appointment — you can drive yourself home afterward!

Nitrous Oxide Sedation is available at all Castlemore Dental Centre locations. Let our team know if you're interested during your booking.
